Security Nodes

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

At Pixel io, security is not an afterthought — it is an architectural principle embedded at every node of our infrastructure. This document outlines the security protocols, encryption layers, and responsible practices we use to protect your data and maintain the integrity of all digital communications.

SSL / TLS Encrypted Firewall Protected No Third-Party Data Sales Zero Spam Policy Secure Messaging Only

1. Transmission Security

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 / TLS 1.3 (Transport Layer Security). This ensures that any information you submit through our contact forms or communication channels — including names, emails, and project specifications — cannot be intercepted or tampered with in transit.

2. Data Storage Protocol

We do not operate a persistent user database. Inquiry data submitted via our contact form is transmitted directly to our secure communication channel (WhatsApp Business) and is never stored on publicly accessible servers. Only authorised Pixel io team members have access to incoming project inquiries.

  • No passwords are stored on our end.
  • No payment information is ever processed through our website.
  • Contact form data is used exclusively to respond to your inquiry.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services on our platform:

  • Google Fonts — Typography delivery. Google Privacy Policy
  • Font Awesome CDN — Icon assets. No personal data is collected by this service.
  • WhatsApp Business (wa.me) — Secure project inquiry routing via end-to-end encrypted messaging.

We do not integrate advertising networks, tracking pixels, or data broker services of any kind.

4. Secure Communication Channels

All client inquiries are routed through WhatsApp Business which uses end-to-end encryption by default. This means only you and our team can read the messages — not even WhatsApp can access the content of your inquiry.

Our official secure contact points are:

  • Email: pixel.io.service26@gmail.com
  • WhatsApp: +880 15754-78716

5. Vulnerability & Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability on our platform or services, we encourage responsible disclosure. Please contact us privately before making any information public so we can address the issue promptly.

To report a security issue, email us at pixel.io.service26@gmail.com with the subject line "Security Disclosure". We will respond within 48 hours and credit researchers who help improve our security posture.

6. Policy Updates

This Security Nodes document may be updated periodically as our infrastructure evolves. Any significant changes will be reflected with a revised effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes your acknowledgment of the revised protocols.
