Telemetry Log

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

Pixel io operates with full transparency. This Telemetry Log documents exactly what information our platform observes, collects, and processes during normal operation — so you always know what is running in the background of your interaction with us.

No Analytics Trackers No Tracking Cookies No User Profiling Zero Data Brokers Performance Only

The table below is a complete index of all data nodes we interact with during a visitor session. Each entry specifies the data type, its purpose, and its collection status on our platform.

Canvas Telemetry (Interactive Background)

Our site features an interactive pixel canvas background powered by vanilla JavaScript. This canvas responds to your cursor movement to create a visual effect. No positional data from your mouse or touch inputs is stored, transmitted, or logged — the interaction is entirely local to your browser session and discarded immediately.

Performance Telemetry

We design our platform to achieve sub-1.2 second load times. To maintain this, we periodically review our own site using tools like:

  • Google PageSpeed Insights — Manual, one-off performance audits. No ongoing data pipeline.
  • Browser DevTools — Internal testing only; no data is captured from live visitors.

We do not run any real-user monitoring (RUM) or automated performance sampling on visitor sessions.

Data Retention Policy

Since we do not operate a persistent database for visitor data:

  • Contact form submissions exist only inside our encrypted WhatsApp Business thread.
  • Newsletter opt-in emails (if any) are managed manually and can be removed on request.
  • There are no automated retention schedules, expiry timers, or archiving pipelines.

To request deletion of any data you've submitted to us, contact pixel.io.service26@gmail.com or message us on WhatsApp. We will action your request within 72 hours.

Log Updates

This Telemetry Log is reviewed and updated whenever the platform's technology stack, third-party dependencies, or data handling practices change. The effective date at the top of this document reflects the most recent revision.
